Nats chart comes from https://github.com/helm/charts/tree/master/stable/nats

By default there is no external service exposed. Once the nodes are up and running you may test it internally with:

curl -i -XPOST http://skyring-node--skyring.skyring.svc:3000/timer -d '{
  "timeout": 6000,
  "data" : "{\"foo\":\"bar\"}",
  "callback": {
    "transport": "http",
    "method": "post",
    "uri": "http://api.someservice.com/hook/timeout"
  }
}'
